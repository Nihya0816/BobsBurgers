What does Bob's Burgers mean to me?

  • It represents the idea of spontaneous events. Nothing in the show ever goes as planned.
  • Bob's Burgers is very uplifting. Whenever thier friends or family are upset, despite their differences, they always come around and uplift each other.
  • Louise Belcher is the brains of the children. She's constantly getting into trouble by using her smarts and techniques.
  • Gene Belcher is more of the silly one. He looks at life as if it's just one big joke. He never likes to take things too seriously because then life wouldn't be as fun.
  • Tina Belcher is an outcast. She's not like the other kids. She's tries to stay out of the way of trouble and she's always trying to be cool by being like everyone else.
